Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does child care cost at Bright Minds?
+
Pricing varies by program and age group. We encourage you to call us at (612) 545-5599 for a personalized quote. We also work with many subsidy and assistance programs to help make quality care accessible for every family.
What are your hours of operation?
+
We are open Monday through Sunday, 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM — including weekends and most holidays. We understand working families need flexibility, and we're here for you.
Do you offer part-time or flexible scheduling?
+
Yes! We offer full-day, part-time, and drop-in options depending on availability. Contact us to discuss a schedule that works for your family's needs.
Do you provide meals and snacks?
+
Yes, we provide nutritious meals and snacks throughout the day following USDA guidelines. Our menus are designed to be healthy, balanced, and kid-friendly. Please inform us of any allergies during enrollment.
What is your staff-to-child ratio?
+
We follow Minnesota state licensing requirements and maintain low ratios: 1:4 for infants, 1:7 for toddlers, and 1:10 for preschool. Our small groups ensure every child receives individual attention.
Do you accept childcare assistance programs?
+
Absolutely. We accept CCAP (Child Care Assistance Program), Child Care Scholarships, and many employer-sponsored childcare benefits. Call us and we'll help you navigate the options available to your family.
