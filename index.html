Who We Are

More Than
Just Child Care

At Bright Minds, we believe every child deserves a space to explore, discover, and thrive. Our dedicated educators create structured, play-based learning experiences tailored to every age group.

Located in the heart of Minneapolis, we've been a trusted partner for families since 2010 — providing safe, nurturing, and enriching care that parents can count on.

Infant Care
